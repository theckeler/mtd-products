The PRO PCS 1900 (Power Collection System) is designed and engineered to keep any crew mowing. It features a 19" diameter impeller housing, the largest of any bagger in the market, and a rectangular upper tube design that enables grass to flow through to the bag without plugging. This 12-Bushel, three bag collection system will outperform any typical bagging unit.

Fits all Pro Z 500, 700 and 900 models with a 54- or 60- Cutting Deck

  • Part of the PRO PCS 1900  (Power Collection System)
  • 12-bushel, three bag collection system
  • 19 in diameter impeller housing
  • 15 in impeller directs grass into the bags with optimal performance
  • Built-in impeller handles allow for easy removal from the unit

Components of PRO PCS 1900 (Power Collection System) are sold separately

  • Three Bag Collection System (59A30060150)
  • 54-in Deck Mounting Kit (59A30061150)
  • 60-in Deck Mounting Kit (59A30062150)
  • Fan-Assist Kit (59A30063150)
  • Frame Weight Kit (59B30037150) is required for all PRO Z lap bar models and is not compatible with PRO Z Steering Wheel Models.
