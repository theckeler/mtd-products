FAQs

How long does the online application take?

The application takes only minutes, and most application decisions are returned within seconds.

Is there an annual fee with the Troy-Bilt credit card?

There is no annual fee to carry the Troy-Bilt credit card.

Can I apply for the Troy-Bilt credit card by mail?

There is no mail-in application. Applications can only be completed online.

How do I make payments on my Troy-Bilt credit card account?

You can make payments on your Troy-Bilt credit card account in 3 convenient ways:

  1. Online with TD's Online Account Management system at www.myonlineaccount.net
  2. Via phone at 1-866-702-4074
  3. Via mail: Troy-Bilt, PO BOX 100114, Columbia SC 29202-3114
How do I contact customer service regarding my Troy-Bilt credit card account?

The customer service number for your Troy-Bilt credit card account is 1-800-808-6950.

Where can I use my Troy-Bilt credit card?

The Troy-Bilt credit card can be used online at Troybilt.com.
