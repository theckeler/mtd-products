Extended Warranty FAQs

  • What products qualify for the Cub Cadet Extended Warranty?✖

  • The Cub Cadet Extended Warranty is available for residential products only. Eligible Cub Cadet products include residential walk-behind mowers (push and self-propelled), snow blowers, and ride-on mowers (garden, lawn, and residential zero-turn).

  • Can the Cub Cadet Extended Warranty be financed with a Cub Cadet unit?✖

  • Yes. The Cub Cadet Extended Warranty can be financed with a Cub Cadet unit.

  • Does the Cub Cadet Extended Warranty cover pickup and delivery costs?✖

  • As an extension of the manufacturer’s warranty, transportation and travel expenses are not covered.

  • What are the benefits for purchasing a Cub Cadet Extended Warranty?✖

  • There are several reasons to purchase a Cub Cadet Extended Warranty:

    • Continued best in class warranty protection sold and administered by Cub Cadet
    • Additional 2 year coverage after the manufacturer's warranty ends

  • Will the Extended Warranty be tied to the model and serial number of the unit?✖

  • Yes, the serial number.

  • If attachments or accessories are purchased with the unit, is the Extended Warranty cost based on the total sale?✖

  • No, the Extended Warranty is tied to the model number of the unit only.

  • Will I receive a confirmation of coverage after purchase?✖

  • Yes, you will receive a confirmation email and welcome letter from Cub Cadet that includes the terms and conditions of the Extended Warranty. An email address must be provided in order to complete the transaction.

  • Can the Extended Warranty be purchased when the manufacturer's warranty expires?✖

  • No, an Extended Warranty can only be purchased up to 2 years after the purchase date of their unit.

  • Where should Cub Cadet units be taken for Extended Warranty service?✖

  • Cub Cadet units should be taken to authorized local Cub Cadet dealers for service.

  • Is there a deductible when using the Cub Cadet Extended Warranty?✖

  • No, there is no deductible under the Cub Cadet Extended Warranty.

  • Can an Extended Warranty be purchased for units previously sold?✖

  • Yes. The Cub Cadet Extended Warranty can be purchased up to 2 years after the sale of a unit.
